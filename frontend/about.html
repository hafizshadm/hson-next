Our Story

How one frustrated marketer built a different kind of agency.

What We Kept Running Into

Three vendors on one brand, and none of them had spoken to each other in weeks. Or a strategy deck copy-pasted from a London campaign, dropped onto Riyadh with a find-and-replace on the city name.

What We Built Instead

One team, accountable for the whole thing — strategy, creative, media, and the build it all points to. The person who wrote the campaign brief is in the same room as the person shipping the website.

The Rule We Still Work By

If we wouldn't be comfortable explaining a recommendation to your face, we don't make it. That's the whole standard — no jargon to hide behind, no strategy we can't defend.

How We're Different

Four decisions everyone else skips.

No hand-offs between vendors

Strategy, design, ads, content, and development sit inside one team, so nothing gets lost in translation between departments.

Arabic and English get equal effort

Neither language is treated as a translation of the other. Separate native-level writers for each language.

We track AEO/GEO because we use it ourselves

We structure our own content — and our clients' — to be legible to AI search tools, not just to Google.

We say no to work that won't move the needle

If a service you're asking for won't actually help your specific goal, we'll tell you before we take the project.

Questions about Hson itself

Is Hson one team, or a network of freelancers stitched together?

One core team. Services are run by specialists who work together daily, not a rotating bench of freelancers assembled per project.

Do you only work with Gulf-based companies, or international brands too?

Both. Most clients are Gulf-based SMEs and regional enterprises, but we also work with international brands entering the region for the first time who need local market judgment they don't have in-house.

What size of business do you typically work with?

Mostly SMEs and mid-sized regional enterprises — big enough to need more than a single freelancer, not so large that an internal marketing department already covers most of this.

How is this different from just hiring an in-house marketer?

An in-house hire gives you one person across ten disciplines. We give you specialists in each — strategy, content, ads, design, and development — coordinated as if they were one hire, often for less than a single senior in-house salary.

What happens if we're not actually a good fit?

We'll say so upfront. If your goals don't match what we're actually good at, we'd rather tell you in the first call than take the project and underdeliver on it.

Who makes the final call on strategy — us or you?

You approve the direction; we own the execution details. We'll push back hard if we think something's a mistake, but the business decisions are always yours to make.

Do you outsource work overseas to cut costs?

No. Our specialists are based in the region and work the same hours you do, which matters more than it sounds for approvals, shoots, and same-day changes.
