Frequently asked questions about working with Hson.

Do you build custom packages, or is it fixed pricing per service?

Custom. Most clients need a mix of two or three services, not all ten — we scope based on the actual goal, not a set menu.

Can you run campaigns in both Arabic and English at the same level of quality?

Yes — this is handled by separate native-level writers for each language rather than one team translating the other's work.

How long before a new client sees results?

Depends on the service. Paid ads and social content show measurable movement within weeks; SEO and AEO/GEO work typically takes a few months to show ranking and citation changes, which we'll be upfront about during scoping.

Can we see examples of past work?

We're building out a public case study library now. In the meantime, book a call and we'll walk you through examples relevant to your industry directly.

Do you only work with businesses in the GCC?

No. We support brands across the GCC from anywhere in the world. Most of the work happens remotely, so where you are based is not a problem.

Who actually does the work — is any of it outsourced?

It's handled in-house by our core team, not rotating freelancers. You'll know who's running your account by name, not by a support ticket queue.

Can you take over mid-campaign from our current agency or freelancers?

Yes — this is one of the most common ways clients come to us. We start with an audit of what's already running so nothing gets dropped during the handover.
